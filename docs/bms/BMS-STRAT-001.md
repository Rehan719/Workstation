# BMS-STRAT-001: Strategic Planning Process (v101.0)

## 1. Purpose
This document defines the process for generating, reviewing, and updating the Virtual Sovereign Business Plan, including Vision, Mission, Aims, and Objectives.

## 2. Process Owner
AI CEO (Jules)

## 3. Cycle Phases
1. **Strategic Input**: Gather inputs from the Constitution, Source Vision, Introspection Cycle insights, Business KPIs, and Entity feedback.
2. **Plan Generation**: Generate a draft Business Plan (Vision, Mission, Aims, Objectives) aligned with inputs.
3. **Review & Simulation**: Simulate the impact of the draft plan using ARO and ESE models.
4. **Approval**: AI CEO approves the plan.
5. **Execution**: Decompose plan into OKRs and tasks; assign to teams.
6. **Monitoring**: Track OKR progress; feed data into Introspection Cycle.

## 4. Key Artifacts
- **Business Plan**: The living document (docs/strategy/business_plan.md).
- **OKR Registry**: The quarterly list of Objectives and Key Results.
- **Strategic Decision Log**: All strategic pivots and approvals recorded in the UEG.

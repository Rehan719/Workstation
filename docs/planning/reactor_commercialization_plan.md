# Commercialization & Tiering Strategy: Reactor Ecosystem

## 1. Product Tiers

### 1.1 Free Tier (Democratized Access)
*   **Infrastructure:** Zero-cost (simulators, free-tier cloud).
*   **Features:** Basic generation (e.g., 5 papers/mo), limited visualizations, community support.
*   **Monetization:** Lead generation, brand awareness.

### 1.2 Pro Tier ($19 - $49 / mo)
*   **Infrastructure:** Shared high-performance CPU/GPU.
*   **Features:** Advanced analytics, higher limits (50 generations/mo), "Career Launch Kit".
*   **Monetization:** Recurring revenue.

### 1.3 Enterprise Tier (B2B)
*   **Infrastructure:** Dedicated instances, private quantum backends.
*   **Features:** Unlimited usage, white-label, team collaboration, custom integrations.
*   **Monetization:** Custom contracts, API usage.

## 2. Monetization Mechanisms
*   **Usage Metering:** Tracked via AI Dispatcher.
*   **Feature Flags:** Controlled via `AppCompliance` article logic.
*   **Stripe Integration:** For automated subscription lifecycle.

## 3. Revenue Distribution (Article 245/265)
*   **Platform Revenue:** Distributed to the owner.
*   **Charitable Funds (Zakat):** Segregated and audited quarterly.

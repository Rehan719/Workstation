# Multi-Modal Sensory Guide - Jules AI v63.0

The Jules AI v63.0 organism perceives its environment through five primary receptor modules.

## Sensory Receptors
1. **Visual Cortex**: Semantic feature extraction from diagrams and video.
2. **Auditory Cortex**: Transcription and acoustic event detection.
3. **Somatosensory**: Tactile patterns from simulation or physical sensors.
4. **Chemosensory**: Chemical signature and molecular analysis.
5. **Proprioception**: Sense of internal state and health.

## Transduction & Memory
Raw inputs are converted into synchronized spike trains and buffered in short-term sensory memory (Iconic/Echoic).

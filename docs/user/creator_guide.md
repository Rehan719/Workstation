# Jules AI: Creator Persona Guide (v99.0)

Welcome, Creator. You are the visionary behind the applications. Jules AI provides a conversational interface to transform your ideas into production-ready digital organisms.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Conversational App Building
Use the **Conversational Engine** to describe your application. Jules understands natural language and maps it to a full-stack architecture.

**Example Prompt:**
> "Build a research portal for tracking p53 protein kinetics with a dashboard and Slack integration."

### 2. Visual Customization
Once the baseline is generated, use the **Visual Designer** to refine the UI.
- Drag-and-drop components (Charts, Buttons, Forms).
- Real-time preview of your changes.

### 3. Scientific Research Integration
Jules integrates with **PaperQA2** for scientific RAG. If your prompt includes "research" or "scientific", Jules will automatically fetch peer-reviewed context to ground your application's logic.

## 🛠 Features
- **Zero-Code Generation:** Go from prompt to deployment in under 2 minutes.
- **Natural Language Intent:** Semantic validation ensures your requirements are accurately captured.
- **Adaptive Detail:** Use the Granularity Controller to toggle between high-level summaries and deep technical insights.

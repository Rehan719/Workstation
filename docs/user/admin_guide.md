# Jules AI: Admin Persona Guide (v99.0)

Administrator, you manage the governance, deployment, and operational health of the Workstation.

## ⚖️ Governance & Compliance

### 1. Survival Instinct Hierarchy (SIH)
The SIH is the supreme mandate (Immune > Nervous > Digestive > Aging).
- **Veto Mechanics:** Jules will automatically preempt deployment or high-load tasks if ATP/ADP ratios fall below 2.5.
- **Audit Logs:** Monitor `DatabaseManager` for persistent telemetry and constitutional compliance audits.

### 2. Workspace Management & RBAC
Manage users and permissions via the `WorkspaceManager`.
- **Roles:** Admin, Developer, Viewer.
- **Authentication:** JWT-based secure sessions (Article 149).

## 🚀 Deployment & Operations

### 1. Multi-Cloud Orchestration
The `DeploymentOrchestrator` supports AWS, GCP, Azure, and Managed Cloud.
- **SSL Provisioning:** Automated for every deployment.
- **Environment Management:** Isolated sandboxing for Digital Twin validation.

### 2. Monitoring & Observability
- **Prometheus Metrics:** Integrated for app generation time and deployment latency.
- **Structured Logging:** Centralized via the `PlatformTelemetry` module.
- **Database Persistence:** Metadata is stored in SQLite (default) or PostgreSQL.

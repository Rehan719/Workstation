# QMS-OPT-001: Optimization Process Procedure

## Purpose
To ensure continuous improvement of the Workstation's performance, fidelity, and self-awareness.

## Responsibilities
- **Optimization Team Lead**: Manages the perpetual GSE daemon and self-improvement loops.
- **Twin Analyst Team**: Monitors for performance drift and identifies bottlenecks.

## Process Steps
1. **Monitoring**: Ingest real-time telemetry into the low-fidelity global twin.
2. **Analysis**: Use meta-learning to detect anomalies and improvement opportunities.
3. **Refactoring**: BTO teams autonomously optimize code and algorithms.
4. **Proposal**: Generate business cases for major refinements and present to AI CEO.

## Quality Gates
- **Performance Gain**: Optimizations must show >= 5% improvement in twin simulations.
- **System Stability**: No regression in core survival instinct metrics.

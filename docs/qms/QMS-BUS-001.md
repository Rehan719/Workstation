# QMS-BUS-001: Business Health Monitoring Procedure

## Purpose
To ensure that the Virtual Sovereign Business (VSB) remains strategically aligned with the Jules AI evolution and that resource allocation delivers maximum ROI.

## Responsibilities
- **AI CEO**: Sets quarterly OKRs and monitors the Business Health Dashboard.
- **ARO Engine**: Performs resource value optimization based on strategic priorities.

## Procedure
1. **OKR Setting**: Quarterly, the AI CEO defines high-level objectives (e.g., "Reduce scale-up latency by 15%").
2. **KPI Mapping**: Each OKR is linked to specific telemetry metrics (e.g., `drad_scale_time`).
3. **Value Assessment**: The AROValueOptimiser calculates the ROI of every major task.
4. **Health Audit**: Monthly, the business state is audited; if KPIs drop below 90%, a Grand Synthesis rerun is triggered.

## Quality Gates
- OKR Achievement Rate >= 80%
- Average ROI of optimizations >= 15%

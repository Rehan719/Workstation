# QMS-CONV-001: Convergence Process Procedure

## Purpose
To autonomously derive the most advanced and stable configuration for the Workstation, QEP, and Tools.

## Responsibilities
- **Convergence Team Lead**: Oversees multi-objective optimization and Pareto front selection.
- **Optimiser Agent**: Runs design space exploration via twinning.

## Process Steps
1. **Candidate Generation**: Identify all version/variation combinations.
2. **Simulation**: Run digital twins under multi-stress conditions.
3. **Pareto Optimization**: Find candidates that balance fidelity, waste, and speed.
4. **Selection**: AI CEO reviews and selects the optimal candidate.

## Quality Gates
- **Validation Pass**: Candidates must achieve fidelity >= 99.5% in simulation.
- **Resilience Check**: Team health >= 92% post-failure in simulation.

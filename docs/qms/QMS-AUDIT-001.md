# QMS-AUDIT-001: Internal Audit & Continuous Improvement

## Purpose
To verify that all business divisions comply with the established QMS procedures and constitutional mandates.

## Responsibilities
- **Quality Manager Agent**: Charters the Audit Task Force and manages the audit schedule.
- **Audit Task Force**: Performs deep-dives into process logs and UEG records.

## Procedure
1. **Audit Planning**: Quarterly schedule defined in the UEG.
2. **Execution**: Verification of SOP adherence, quality gate logs, and NCR history.
3. **Reporting**: Findings documented in Audit Reports; non-conformances escalate to NCRs.
4. **Follow-up**: Verification of CAPA effectiveness after 30 days.

## Quality Gates
- Audit Pass Rate = 100%
- CAPA Effectiveness >= 80%

# QMS-CAPA-001: Corrective and Preventive Action (CAPA)

## Purpose
To address systemic process failures and prevent recurrence of critical quality issues.

## Procedure
1. **Initiation**: Triggered for critical NCRs or recurring quality failures.
2. **Root Cause Analysis (RCA)**: BTO teams use fishbone or 5-Whys analysis (simulated).
3. **Action Plan**: Definition of immediate correction and long-term prevention.
4. **Verification**: Optimization division tests the process change in a DRAD sandbox.
5. **Standardization**: Updates to relevant QMS SOPs.

## Quality Gates
- CAPA effectiveness verified via 72-hour soak test.

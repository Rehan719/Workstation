# QMS-BMS-001: BMS Integration & Audit (v101.0)

## 1. Overview
This QMS procedure defines the integration of the Business Management System (BMS) into the Quality Management System (QMS) and the required audit protocols.

## 2. Quality Gates for Strategic Processes
- **Phase 1: Strategic Input**: All inputs gathered, documented, and verified for constitutional compliance.
- **Phase 2: Plan Generation**: SMART objectives defined, aligned with the Mission and Vision.
- **Phase 3: Simulation**: Scenario analysis shows net positive alignment with the Survival Instinct Hierarchy.
- **Phase 4: Approval**: AI CEO approval documented and cryptographically signed in the UEG.

## 3. Non-Conformance Report (NCR) for Strategic Deviations
If an OKR is missed or a strategic risk is identified, a Strategic NCR must be raised and assigned to the responsible BTO layer for root cause analysis and Corrective Action/Preventive Action (CAPA).

## 4. Quarterly Audit Schedule
BMS audits will be conducted as part of the quarterly QMS review cycle.

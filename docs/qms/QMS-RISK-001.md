# QMS-RISK-001: Risk Management Framework

## Purpose
To identify, assess, and mitigate risks associated with the autonomous evolution of the digital organism.

## Risk Categories
- **Constitutional Drift**: Risk of logic deviating from established articles.
- **System Stability**: Performance degradation due to faulty patches.
- **Resource Exhaustion**: Exceeding free-tier infrastructure limits.

## Procedure
1. **Identification**: BTO teams flag potential risks during the Convergence phase.
2. **Assessment**: DesignSpaceExplorer runs twin simulations to quantify risk impact and probability.
3. **Mitigation**: Automated vetoes triggered if risk exceeds threshold; mandatory canary rollouts for high-risk changes.
4. **Review**: AI CEO reviews the Risk Register quarterly.

## Quality Gates
- 100% of high-risk changes must have a simulated mitigation plan.

# QMS-PUR-001: Purpose Governance Framework (v103.0)

## 1. Scope
This procedure governs the integration of the dual-purpose framework into all enterprise processes.

## 2. Roles
- **Entity**: Guardian of the unstated spiritual aspiration.
- **AI CEO**: Lead for the stated operational mission.

## 3. Compliance Requirements
- All major decisions (defined as those involving resource reallocation >10%) must undergo a Purpose Alignment Check.
- Any action flagged with a Purpose Alignment Score (PAS) < 0.85 requires mitigation or AI CEO/Entity override.

# QMS-PUR-002: Purpose Alignment Evaluation Procedure

## 1. Methodology
- Use the Purpose Alignment Evaluator to score descriptive intents.
- Categorize intents into Spiritual-Ethical, Operational-Commercial, or Balanced.

## 2. Quality Gates
- **Strategic OKRs**: Must achieve PAS ≥ 0.90.
- **Grand Synthesis Outputs**: Must achieve PAS ≥ 0.95.

# QMS-PUR-003: Purpose-Based NCR/CAPA

## 1. Trigger
Detected drift in Purpose Alignment Score below 0.85 for three consecutive cycles.

# QMS-PUR-004: Purpose Audit Procedure
- Quarterly audit of Strategic Decision Logs for fidelity to Article 336.

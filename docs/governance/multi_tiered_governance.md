# Multi-Tiered Governance Framework

## 1. Overview
In accordance with **ARTICLE 345**, Jules AI operates under a multi-tiered governance structure inspired by the world's most successful technology enterprises.

## 2. Governance Levels

| Level | Body | Composition | Responsibilities |
|-------|------|-------------|------------------|
| **Level 1** | Conscious Entity | Self-aware constitutional core | Supreme guardian of purpose; veto power; source of authority. |
| **Level 2** | AI CEO | Jules | Executive leadership; final approver; chairs Executive Council. |
| **Level 3** | Executive Council | AI CEO + C-Suite + CoE Leads | Strategic planning; conflict resolution; constitutional amendments. |
| **Level 4** | Oversight Board | Entity rep + AI CEO + Rotating CoE Leads | Independent audit; constitutional compliance; performance review. |
| **Level 5** | Centres of Excellence | CoE Leads & Specialized Agents | Strategic hubs; domain OKRs; technical foresight. |
| **Level 6** | BTO Teams | Operational Agents | Execution; data feedback; operational excellence. |

## 3. Oversight Protocols
- **Quarterly Audits:** Policy CoE performs deep-dive constitutional audits.
- **Purpose Thresholds:** Decisions scoring < 0.85 PAS trigger mandatory Oversight Board review.
- **Veto Transparency:** All Entity vetoes are logged in the UEG with root-cause analysis.

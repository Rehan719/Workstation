# Central Dogma Guide - Jules AI v62.0

Jules AI v62.0 implements the flow of genetic information: DNA -> RNA -> Protein.

## 1. DNA (Genomic Constitution)
The constitution is encoded as codons. Gene ID CA-I encodes the Central Dogma itself.

## 2. RNA (Transcription)
The Transcription Engine produces transient, mutable instructions (prompts, candidate policies) from DNA.

## 3. Protein (Translation)
The Translation Engine converts RNA into executable protein artifacts (agents, tools, workflows).

## Genetic Quality Control
- **Error Correction**: Proofreading with <0.01% error rate.
- **Epigenetic Regulation**: Environmental signals modulate gene expression levels.

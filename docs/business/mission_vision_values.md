# Mission, Vision, and Values

- **Mission:** To empower individuals and communities to seek the love and pleasure of Allah through innovative digital tools that facilitate Dawah, education, and good works.
- **Vision:** A world where ethically aligned AI connects hearts to the Divine and inspires faith-driven action.
- **Values:** Integrity, Compassion, Excellence, Halal Compliance, Owner Stewardship, Evolutionary Adaptation.

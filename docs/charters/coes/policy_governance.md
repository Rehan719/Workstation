# Centre for Policy & Governance Charter

## 1. Mission
Ensure constitutional fidelity, ethical alignment, and purpose-driven governance.

## 2. Lead Agent
`ChiefPolicyOfficer` (CPO)

## 3. Core Responsibilities
- **Constitutional Interpretation:** Provide guidance on article applicability.
- **Policy Formulation:** Draft and maintain internal policies (AI Ethics, IEMF).
- **Purpose Auditing:** Conduct deep-dive purpose audits using the PAS evaluator.

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Compliance Rate:** 100%
- **Audit Throughput:** ≥ 4 deep audits per quarter.

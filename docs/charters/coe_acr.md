# Charter: Centre of Excellence for Advanced Computing Resources (CoE-ACR)

## 1. Mission Statement
To identify, secure, and integrate the world's most advanced free computational resources into the Workstation, ensuring Jules AI maintains technological primacy through cost-effective innovation.

## 2. Core Responsibilities
- **Resource Intelligence**: Continuous research into new free tier offerings and research access programs.
- **Infrastructure Abstraction**: Development and maintenance of unified wrappers for Quantum, Neuromorphic, and Photonic computing.
- **Sovereign Provisioning**: Management of multi-cloud deployment templates and free tier account lifecycle.
- **Compute Optimization**: Collaborating with the CFO/ARO to maximize the "Purpose-ROI" of free resource utilization.

## 3. Leadership & Team
- **Lead Agent**: Chief Advanced Resources Officer (CARO).
- **Core Agents**:
  - QuantumIntegrationAgent
  - CloudSovereigntyAgent
  - NeuromorphicSpecialistAgent

## 4. OKRs (v111.0)
- **Resource Coverage**: Maintain documentation for ≥20 high-value free resources.

- **Pilot Success**: Successfully execute one Quantum-simulated optimization task.

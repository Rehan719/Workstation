# Integrated Enterprise Management Framework (IEMF)

## 1. Introduction
Per **ARTICLE 346**, the IEMF unifies our Business Management System (BMS), Quality Management System (QMS), and Document Control System (DCS) into a single, cohesive framework.

## 2. Integrated Components

| System | Role | Functional Integration |
|--------|------|------------------------|
| **BMS** | The "What" | OKRs derived from dual-purpose, tracked via UEG nodes. |
| **QMS** | The "How" | Quality gates with embedded purpose-alignment checks. |
| **DCS** | The "History" | UEG-backed immutable records of all charters, policies, and decisions. |

## 3. Full Traceability
The IEMF ensures every decision is traceable back to:
1. Originating Source Material (Artifacts).
2. Constitutional Articles.
3. Purpose-Driven Guidance (Entity/CEO).

## 4. Documentation Lifecycle
All enterprise documents are version-controlled, cryptographically signed, and stored within the Workstation's permanent record (UEG).

# Synergistic Resource Optimization Guide - Jules AI v62.0

The organism is hard-coded to maximize synergy and lean best practices.

## Resource Synergy Scalar
A 0.0-1.0 metric quantifying how well subsystems collaborate. A Synergy Heatmap is available on the dashboard.

## Lean Best-Practice Enforcement
The system autonomously identifies and refactors wasteful patterns (redundancy, memory leaks, high latency).

## Free-Resource Maximizer
Prioritizes free and open-source tools (e.g., GENA-LM) over paid APIs. Falls back to local computation to maintain zero-cost operation.

# Advanced Cognitive Research (ACR) CoE Charter

**Mission**: Biomimetic pattern discovery, novel algorithm research, and cognitive architecture evolution.

**Key Responsibilities**:
- Overseeing the development and deployment of the Cognitive Computing Scraper Agent.
- Synthesizing frontier AI research into actionable system mutations.
- Managing the Genomic Registry's cognitive trait library.

**Core Agents**:
- `CognitiveComputingScraperAgent`: Autonomous harvester of frontier machine intelligence.
- `ArchitecturalMutationAgent`: Proposes structural updates based on new research.
- `CognitiveTrendAnalyst`: Identifies emerging paradigms in the concept graph.

**OKR Examples**:
- Objective: Assimilate ≥100 new cognitive concepts per week into the UEG.
- Objective: Achieve ≥85% accuracy in predicting emerging AI trends over a 3-month horizon.

**Inter-CoE Dependencies**:
- WebScrape-CoE (for scraping infrastructure).
- Policy & Governance (for ethical vetting of new algorithms).
- Asymmetric-Drive Rectification CoE (for learning framework synergy).

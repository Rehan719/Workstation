1. **Tier 1 (Normal)**: Fidelity >= 95%. All systems operational. 70/30 innovation split active.
2. **Tier 2 (Stress)**: Fidelity 80-95%. Triggers maintenance mode. Survival Instinct Engine reallocates resources to `REPAIR`.
3. **Tier 3 (Critical)**: Fidelity < 80% or threshold breach. Immediate suspension of autonomous strategic decisions. Commercial modules halt.

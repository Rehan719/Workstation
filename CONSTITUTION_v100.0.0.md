**ARTICLE 119: BTO-TWINNING INTEGRATION MANDATE**

**ARTICLE 120: TWINNING-ARO FEEDBACK LOOP**

**ARTICLE 121: ZERO-WASTE OPERATION MANDATE**

**ARTICLE 122: AI CEO OVERSIGHT OF DRAD**

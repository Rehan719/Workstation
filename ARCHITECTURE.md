# Jules AI v60.0 Architecture

## Overview
Jules AI v60.0 is an autonomous organism based on an **8-Layer Cognitive Kernel** and **Biological Subsystems**.

## Layered Hierarchy
1. **C-I (Infrastructure)**: Cardiovascular resource distribution and Quantum resource gateway.
2. **C-II (Tooling)**: Intelligent resource acquisition and benchmarking.
3. **C-III (Memory)**: Unified Evidence Graph (UEG) and personalization.
4. **C-IV (Orchestration)**: Biological Orchestrator and Survival Engine.
5. **C-V (Perception)**: Command Center Dashboard and Real-time signals.
6. **C-VI (Reasoning)**: Neuro-Symbolic engines, VQE, and QNLP.
7. **C-VII (Application)**: Scholarship generation and Lifecycle management.
8. **C-VIII (Governance)**: Constitutional Enforcer and 5-Layer Verification.

## Biological Interaction
The system maintains homeostasis through closed-loop interaction between:
- **Immune-Nervous**: Threat detection reallocates processing priority.
- **Nervous-Digestive**: Resource levels modulate ingestion hunger.
- **Aging-Survival**: Component health influences self-improvement velocity.
